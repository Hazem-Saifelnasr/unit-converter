# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-27

Unit Converter is an offline Android utility app.

## Data Collection

This app does not collect personal data.

## Data Sharing

This app does not share user data with third parties.

## Accounts And Sign-In

This app does not require an account, sign-in, or user registration.

## Internet And Backend Services

This app does not require cloud services, analytics, advertising SDKs, or external APIs for core functionality.

## Local Storage

The app stores recent conversion history only on the device. This local data is used only to support the app experience and is not transmitted externally.

## Children

This app is a general-purpose utility app and is not specifically directed to children.
